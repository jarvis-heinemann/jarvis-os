# ClaimHaus Demo Script (15 Minutes)

**Purpose:** Run effective pilot demos that convert  
**Audience:** Debt Buyers, Portfolio Managers, Collections Directors  
**Goal:** Book follow-up meeting or close pilot on the spot

---

## Pre-Demo Checklist (5 min before)

- [ ] Test screen sharing
- [ ] Open ClaimHaus demo environment
- [ ] Prepare sample portfolio ($5M dataset)
- [ ] Have case study ready to share
- [ ] Mute notifications
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Prepare water/coffee

## Demo Flow (15 minutes)

### 1. Introduction & Agenda (2 min)

**Say:**
> "Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time today. I'm Gabriel Heinemann, founder of ClaimHaus.
> 
> Today I'll show you how our platform helps debt buyers like [Company] achieve faster collections and automated compliance.
> 
> We'll cover:
> - Quick overview of ClaimHaus (2 min)
> - Live demo of the platform (10 min)
> - Q&A and next steps (3 min)
> 
> Sound good?"

**Wait for:** Confirmation

**Then:**
> "Great. Before we dive in, I'm curious - what's your biggest challenge with portfolio management right now?"

**Listen for:** Pain points (write these down)

---

### 2. Problem Alignment (2 min)

**Say:**
> "That makes sense. Most debt buyers we talk to face similar challenges:
> 
> - **Manual processes** → Slow collections, missed opportunities
> - **Compliance burden** → Hours spent on audit prep
> - **Poor visibility** → No real-time portfolio insights
> 
> Does that resonate with what you're seeing at [Company]?"

**Wait for:** Agreement/validation

**Then:**
> "That's exactly what we built ClaimHaus to solve. Let me show you."

---

### 3. Platform Demo (10 min)

#### 3a. Dashboard Overview (2 min)

**Screen:** Main dashboard

**Say:**
> "Here's the ClaimHaus dashboard. This gives you real-time visibility into your entire portfolio.
> 
> You can see:
> - Total portfolio value ($5M in this example)
> - Collection rate trends (15% → 21% with ClaimHaus)
> - Compliance score (100% - audit ready)
> 
> Everything updates in real-time, so you always know where you stand."

**Click:** Show live updates

**Ask:** "What metrics matter most to you day-to-day?"

---

#### 3b. Portfolio View (3 min)

**Screen:** Portfolio list

**Say:**
> "Let's look at individual debt positions.
> 
> [Click on a debt position]
> 
> Each debt is tokenized as a digital asset. You can see:
> - Original balance, current balance
> - Collection rate and status
> - Last payment date
> - Compliance score
> 
> [Point to token ID]
> 
> This token ID means every action is tracked and auditable. You can prove exactly what happened, when, and why."

**Scroll:** Show more positions

**Ask:** "How do you currently track individual debt positions?"

---

#### 3c. Compliance Center (3 min)

**Screen:** Compliance tab

**Say:**
> "This is where ClaimHaus really shines - the compliance center.
> 
> [Click Evidence Packets]
> 
> Every action generates an evidence packet - signed, timestamped, audit-ready.
> 
> [Click Export]
> 
> When you need to prepare for an audit, just click Export. In 8 hours or less, you have everything you need.
> 
> [Show policy automation]
> 
> You can also define rules - like 'Flag any collection over $10,000' - and the system enforces them automatically."

**Ask:** "How long does it take you to prepare for a compliance audit right now?"

**Listen for:** Pain (probably 40+ hours)

**Then:**
> "With ClaimHaus, it's under 8 hours. That's an 80% reduction."

---

#### 3d. Analytics & ROI (2 min)

**Screen:** Analytics tab

**Say:**
> "Finally, let's look at analytics.
> 
> [Show collection performance]
> 
> You can track collection performance over time, see which strategies work best, and identify opportunities.
> 
> [Show ROI calculator]
> 
> We also have an ROI calculator. Based on your portfolio size and current collection rate, ClaimHaus can deliver [calculate based on their numbers].
> 
> For a $50M portfolio, that's typically an additional $3M in collections per year."

**Ask:** "What would an extra $3M in collections mean for [Company]?"

**Screen:** Share screen or send PDF

**Say:**

> 
> [Similar debt buyer] was facing the same challenges you mentioned:
> - [Pain point 1 they mentioned]
> - [Pain point 2 they mentioned]
>

> - **38% faster collections** ($1.2M improvement)

**Ask:** "Does this sound like something that could work for [Company]?"

### 5. Next Steps (3 min)

**Say:**
> "Based on what we discussed, here's what I'd recommend:
> 
> [If they showed interest]
> Let's start with a 90-day pilot. We'll tokenize up to $5M of your portfolio, and you'll see results in the first 30 days.
> 
> The pilot is $5,000 one-time, and if it doesn't pay for itself, you're not obligated to continue.
> 
> [If they have questions]
> What questions do you have?"
> 
> [Address questions]
> 
> **Close:**
> "What would be the best next step for you?
> - Start the pilot this week?
> - Schedule a follow-up with your team?
> - Send you more information?"

**Wait for:** Decision or next step

**If they want pilot:**
> "Great! I'll send over the pilot agreement today. We can have you onboarded by [Day] and see results within 30 days."

**If they need follow-up:**
> "No problem. Who else should be involved in this decision? I'd be happy to do a group demo."

**If they need info:**
> "I'll send over a case study and ROI calculator. Can we schedule a follow-up for [Day]?"

## Objection Handling

### "We already have a system"

**Say:**
> "That's great - most of our clients did too. ClaimHaus isn't replacing your entire system, it's adding a tokenization layer that gives you real-time visibility and automated compliance.
> 
> Can I show you how it would integrate with your current setup?"

---

### "We don't have budget right now"

**Say:**
> "I understand. The pilot is $5K one-time, and most clients see 3-5x ROI in the first 90 days.
> 
> If it doesn't pay for itself, you're not obligated to continue.
> 
> What if we started with a free sample tokenization to show you the value?"

---

### "We're too busy"

**Say:**
> "That's exactly why you need ClaimHaus. The setup takes 48 hours, and then you'll save 10+ hours per week on compliance and reporting.
> 
> Can we schedule a 15-minute demo so you can see how it works?"

---

### "We need to think about it"

**Say:**
> "Absolutely. What specific questions do you have? I'm happy to send over a case study or schedule a call with one of our existing clients.
> 
> What would help you make a decision?"

---

### "How is this different from [Competitor]?"

**Say:**
> "Great question. ClaimHaus is the only platform that offers:
> 
> 1. **True tokenization** - Every debt becomes a digital asset
> 2. **Automated compliance** - Evidence packets generated automatically
> 3. **Real-time visibility** - Live dashboard, not monthly reports
> 
> Most competitors are just CRM systems with reporting. Can I show you the difference in a quick demo?"

## Success Metrics

### Good Demo Indicators
- ✅ They asked 3+ questions
- ✅ They shared specific pain points
- ✅ They asked about pricing/timeline
- ✅ They want to involve others
- ✅ They schedule follow-up

### Bad Demo Indicators
- ❌ No questions asked
- ❌ Didn't share pain points
- ❌ Talked about budget constraints
- ❌ No follow-up scheduled
- ❌ "We'll get back to you"

---

## Tips

1. **Listen more than you talk** (60/40 rule)
2. **Customize to their pain points** (reference early in demo)
3. **Show ROI early and often** (tie features to money)
4. **Ask for the next step** (don't leave it open-ended)
5. **Follow up fast** (within 1 hour)
