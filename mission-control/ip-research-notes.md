## Timeline & Deadlines

### Provisional to Utility
- **12 months** to file non-provisional (continuation)
- Can file multiple continuations within 12 months
- Priority date preserved from provisional filing

### Office Actions
- **3 months** initial response time
- **3-month extension** available (first) - $47 micro
- **3-month extension** available (second) - $138 micro
- **6 months total** maximum

### Patent Term
- **20 years** from earliest effective filing date
- Maintenance fees keep patent alive

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Not yet filed |

| Pending | Under examination |
| Granted | Patent issued |
| Maintained | Maintenance fee paid |
| Abandoned | Not pursued / response missed |
