## 💡 Best Practices

1. **Personalization:** Always include company name + specific trigger
2. **Brevity:** Keep emails under 150 words
3. **One CTA:** Single call-to-action per email
4. **Mobile-friendly:** Most emails read on phones
5. **Follow-up timing:** 3, 7, 14 days works best
6. **Sign-off:** Use first name only (more personal)
