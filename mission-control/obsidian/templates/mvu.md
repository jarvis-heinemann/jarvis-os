## What is an MVU?

Not an MVP. An MVU — **Minimum Viable Universe**.

The smallest version of your world that:
- ✅ Demonstrates the core logic
- ✅ Proves the architecture
- ✅ Has basic roles and economy
- ✅ Can grow on its own

**An MVU is NOT:**
- ❌ The full platform
- ❌ All planned features
- ❌ Perfect design
- ❌ Complete automation
- ❌ The entire galaxy

**An MVU IS:**
- ✅ The seed crystal
- ✅ The smallest self-contained world
- ✅ The proof of concept
- ✅ The foundation for expansion

## Checklist Before Shipping MVU

- [ ] Core loop works end-to-end
- [ ] At least one user role can operate
- [ ] Basic value exchange works
- [ ] System can handle 10x users
- [ ] Success metrics are trackable
- [ ] You've tested it yourself
- [ ] At least one external user tested it

---

## Post-MVU: What Comes Next

**If MVU succeeds:**
1. Gather data from usage
2. Identify expansion modules
3. Prioritize based on leverage
4. Add ONE module at a time

**If MVU fails:**
1. Identify WHY it failed
2. Pivot core loop or abandon
3. Don't add features to fix structural problems

---

**Remember:** The goal is not to build the universe. The goal is to build the *seed* that grows into the universe.

Build small. Ship fast. Let it grow.
